\paragraph{Regression coefficient scaling.}
Estimated $\beta$ for $P_{01}$ quantifies the change in PHQ-9 score per one-unit increase in the raw transition probability.
Coefficients on z-scored continuous covariates correspond to the change in PHQ-9 per one standard deviation increase in the respective predictor, holding other model terms fixed.
