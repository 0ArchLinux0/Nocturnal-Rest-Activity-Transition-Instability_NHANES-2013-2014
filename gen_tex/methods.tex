% Covariate scaling for PHQ-9 OLS models (see final_cohort_phq9_ols_models.py)
\paragraph{Covariate scaling.}
Continuous covariates (age, BMI, poverty--income ratio, self-reported total sleep time, and the actigraphy-based WASO proxy) were standardized (z-scored) using the mean and standard deviation of each variable computed within the analysis sample prior to modeling.
The physical instability metric $P_{01}$ was retained in its original scale (a transition probability between 0 and 1) to facilitate clinical interpretability of the absolute change in rest-to-active transitions associated with each unit increase in $P_{01}$.
Sex (NHANES \texttt{RIAGENDR}) was entered as an unscaled binary/categorical code.
